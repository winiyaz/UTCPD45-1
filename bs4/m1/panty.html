Platforms : Arcade, PSX, N64, PC, GBC

Detailed Information

Available On:

- Arcade
- PC
- Play Station
- Nintendo 64
- GameBoy Color

Release Dates

- Arcade, Play Station, N64 and GameBoy Color - 1997
- PC - 06.1998

Playable Characters

- Arcade, PC, Play Station, N64 - 15 (Kai, Raiden, Shinnok, Liu Kang, Reptile, Scorpion, Jax, Reiko, Johnny Cage, Jarek, Tanya, Fujin, Sub-Zero, Quan Chi, Sonya)
- GameBoy Color - 8 (Raiden, Quan Chi, Fujin, Liu Kang, Sub-Zero, Reiko, Tanya, Scorpion)

- Arcade - Meat
- PC - Goro, Noob Saibot and Meat

- none

Bosses

- Arcade - Shinnok, Quan Chi

- GameBoy Color - Shinnok

- PC - Goro's Lair, The Well, Elder Gods, The Tomb, Wind World, Reptile's Lair, Shaolin Temple, Living Forest, The Prison, Ice Pit, Skull (Patch needed)
- Play Station, N64 - Goro's Lair, The Well, Elder Gods, The Tomb, Wind World, Reptile's Lair, Shaolin Temple, Living Forest, The Prison, Ice Pit
- GameBoy Color - Living Forest, Reptile's Lair, Elder Gods, Ice Pit, Shaolin Temple
